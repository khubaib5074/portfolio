PROFILE

As a passionate aspiring researcher and mechanical engineering student, I am continuously exploring the intersection of robotics, additive manufacturing, and process optimization. Currently, I am working on optimizing the parameters of additively manufactured AlSi10Mg alloys, and developing cutting-edge applications for the Niryo Ned2 robotic arm to enhance its capabilities.
